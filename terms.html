Security is a shared responsibility. You are the sole custodian of:

  • Routing IDs: Your account is tied to a deterministic hash. Protect your human username and your mnemonic to maintain control over your Routing ID.

4. Active Decay & Ephemeral Data Policy

By using this service, you acknowledge and agree to its ephemeral nature:

  • Server-Side TTL: Any message not delivered within 30 days is physically and permanently destroyed by the database engine on the relay server.
  • Local Active Decay: You may manually choose a Retention Policy (1 Hour, 24 Hours, 30 Days). I provide an "Active Decay" engine that wakes up hourly to securely scrub and physically purge expired data from your device.
  • Forensic Shredding: Manual deletions and automated purges utilize a two-stage wipe (overwrite then delete) followed by a database VACUUM to ensure no forensic remnants remain on the disk.

5. Logout Protocols

I provide two distinct methods for ending a session:

  • Standard Logout: Clears session tokens but preserves Signal Identity Keys to ensure your cryptographic identity remains stable for your contacts.
  • Secure Wipe & Logout: A "Scorched Earth" option that physically destroys all cryptographic keys and clears the local database vault. Use this option only if you intend to leave no trace of your existence on the device.
