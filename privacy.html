Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Zero-Knowledge Disclosure: This application is developed by an independent solo developer in Europe. It is designed so that the developer has no technical means to access, read, or decrypt your messages.

1. Data We Do NOT Collect

To ensure maximum privacy, we do not require or collect:

  • Phone numbers or Email addresses
  • Real names or contact lists
  • Browser history or location data

2. Data We Process (GDPR Compliance)

Under the GDPR, we process the minimum data necessary to provide the service:

  • UUID: A randomly generated identifier for your account.
  • Public Keys: Necessary to establish the Signal Protocol handshake.
  • IP Addresses: Processed ephemerally to route data; we do not store logs of your IP address.

3. Encryption & Security

All communication is End-to-End Encrypted (E2EE) using the Signal Protocol (X3DH and Double Ratchet). Private keys never leave your device. We cannot comply with requests to provide message content because we do not possess the keys to decrypt it.

4. Data Retention

Messages are deleted from our server immediately upon successful delivery. Undelivered messages are automatically purged after 30 days.

5. Report Abuse (DSA Article 16)

In compliance with the EU Digital Services Act, users may report User IDs for illegal activity via the in-app reporting tool or by emailing our Point of Contact. We will review reports and may terminate service for specific IDs, but we cannot access message content to verify claims.
