1. GDPR Article 11 Processing

In accordance with Art. 11 GDPR, SecureMessenger is designed for anonymity. We are not required to identify natural persons to satisfy data subject rights. We do not collect names, phone numbers, or emails. Because we cannot identify you, we cannot fulfill "Right to Access" requests unless you provide your private keys—which we never possess.

2. German TDDDG Compliance

Pursuant to § 25 TDDDG, we do not store or access information on your terminal equipment that is not strictly necessary for the technical provision of the messaging service. Zero tracking cookies are used. Local storage is strictly functional and encrypted.

3. Rights of the Data Subject (Art. 15-21 GDPR)

You have the right to access, rectification, and erasure of your data. Due to our Zero-Knowledge architecture, the Scorched Earth Protocol within the app serves as your primary tool to exercise your Right to Erasure (Art. 17) instantly and locally.

4. Data Protection Officer

Given the zero-data nature of our processing, a formal DPO is not required under Art. 37 GDPR. All inquiries should be directed to the official contact listed above.
